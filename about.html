Who am I?

Hello! My name is Thomas (Tom) McCluskey. I'm a multimedia artist currently based in Brooklyn who is currently a student at NYU persuing a BFA in Interactive Media.

I'm currently interested in Motion and UX Design, but I love exploring other mediums of creation such as 3D, Digital Fabrication, and more. I currently work at the NYU MakerSpace as a Teaching assistant, and am also part of the Sustainability team at the design lab.

My favorite things to do are to go hiking, spend time in nature, and hang out with friends.
